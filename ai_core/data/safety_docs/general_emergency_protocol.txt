CRISISBRIDGE AI - GENERAL EMERGENCY & UNKNOWN DISASTER PROTOCOL

This protocol applies to ANY major emergency, disaster, catastrophe, crash (e.g., plane crash, vehicle impact), structural collapse, or unknown critical event that is not explicitly covered in other specific manuals. 

If a guest or staff member reports an immediate, life-threatening emergency or panic situation, follow these Universal Emergency Fallback Steps:

1. REMAIN CALM AND ASSESS
- Do not panic. Determine your immediate safety.
- If you are in immediate danger, move away from the threat.

2. EVACUATE THE AREA
- Leave the building or immediate area immediately using the nearest marked exit.
- NEVER use elevators during an unknown emergency or structural event. Always use the stairs.
- Do not return to your room for personal belongings.

3. HELP OTHERS (IF SAFE)
- Assist guests, the elderly, or those with mobility issues if you can do so without endangering yourself.
- Guide people toward the designated assembly points outside the hotel.

4. CONTACT AUTHORITIES
- Call 911 (or local emergency services) immediately once you are in a safe location.
- Inform the hotel front desk or management about the nature of the emergency and your location.

5. AWAIT INSTRUCTIONS
- Do not re-enter the building until emergency responders or hotel management declare it is safe.
- Await further communication via the CrisisBridge system or emergency personnel.

Remember: In any unknown crisis, the priority is preservation of life. Evacuate, avoid elevators, and seek a safe open space.
